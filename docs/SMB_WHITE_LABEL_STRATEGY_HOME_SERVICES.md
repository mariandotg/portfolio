# Home Services Vertical Strategy (Adapted to Reach $5k MRR)

## Niche Chosen
- Home service SMBs: HVAC, plumbing, electrical, and related local service operators.
- Focus profile: `owner-led companies with 3-20 field techs` in one metro area.

## Why This Niche
- Large and active market with ongoing digital adoption.
- Clear, expensive pain around lead leakage, slow quote follow-up, and inconsistent rebooking.
- Lower compliance friction than medical verticals, faster to implement and iterate.

Reference signals:
- https://www.getjobber.com/home-service-reports/feb-2025/
- https://www.census.gov/library/stories/2025/04/economic-census-geographic-stats.html
- https://www.nfib.com/news/press-release/new-nfib-survey-small-business-optimism-recedes-in-february/

## Product Definition
## Product Name (working)
- `ServiceFlow OS` (white-label per client).

## Core Problem Solved
- “You are losing booked jobs because inquiries, quotes, and follow-ups are inconsistent.”

## What the Product Includes
- Lead intake inbox (forms, calls, messages).
- Missed-call recovery workflow (auto text + callback queue).
- Estimate follow-up automation (nudge sequence until booked or closed-lost).
- Booking/dispatch-lite board for office staff.
- Post-job review request and referral prompts.
- Maintenance plan renewal reminders (for recurring revenue services).

## What It Does Not Include
- Full ERP/accounting replacement.
- Fully custom dispatch engine for enterprise fleets.
- Unlimited bespoke UI/features in base plan.

## Business Model
## Revenue Structure
- One-time setup fee.
- Monthly subscription for hosting, maintenance, support, and minor edits.
- Paid add-ons for advanced custom work.

## Pricing
- `Setup`: `$2,000` standard (up to 2 service lines, 1 brand, 2 users).
- `Launch`: `$499/mo`
- Hosting, monitoring, updates, email support, monthly report.
- `Growth`: `$749/mo`
- Launch + faster SLA + monthly optimization call + 1 workflow tweak/mo.
- `Add-ons`:
- Additional service line: `$300 setup`.
- Custom integration: `$1,000+`.
- Extra automation pack: `$250/mo`.

## Path to $5k MRR
- Option A (cleanest):
- `7 Growth clients x $749 = $5,243 MRR`.
- Option B (mixed):
- `5 Growth ($3,745) + 3 Launch ($1,497) = $5,242 MRR`.

Setup cash during ramp:
- 8 clients x `$2,000` = `$16,000` one-time to fund acquisition and product hardening.
